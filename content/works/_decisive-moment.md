While working at the AFP, I helped the "Interactive" department creating this tool, which aims at helping journalists extract a few seconds from a video, without having to use complex or costly tooling.

- In browser (nothing to install, can be used from any device as long as they can log in)
- Designed as a delibaretly simple and "limited" tool, to make it easy to use and have a small amount of descisions to take

Co-designed the UI with Mathieu Grondin (Black Stain Studio), who also did the UX research and investigations internally at the AFP.
